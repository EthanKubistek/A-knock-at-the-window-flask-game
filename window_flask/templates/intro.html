A knock at the Widow is a text based python game you will be able move room to room and have 3 choices you can make in each room. There is a killer in your house and you have to move though the rooms and hopefully avoid the killer with with your choices. You start by hearing a knock on your window followed by a crash now you just have to surive. good luck.
